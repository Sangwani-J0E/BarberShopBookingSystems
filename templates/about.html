About TIMO FIRST CLASS

At TIMO FIRST CLASS Barber Shop, we’re more than just a place to get a haircut—we’re a destination for quality grooming and exceptional experiences. Our mission is to provide top-tier services at affordable prices, ensuring every client leaves looking and feeling their best.

With a focus on hygiene and precision, our highly experienced barbers bring skill and passion to every cut, trim, and style. Whether you’re after a classic look or something bold and modern, we’ve got you covered.
